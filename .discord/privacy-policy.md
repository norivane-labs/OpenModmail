# OpenModmail Bot – Privacy Policy

OpenModmail App respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights.

1. **Information Collected**
   - The Bot may collect basic information required for functionality, including:
     - Discord username and ID
     - Messages sent through the Bot
     - Server IDs where the Bot is active

2. **Use of Information**
   - Collected data is used solely to provide the Bot’s functionality.
   - We do not sell, rent, or trade user information to third parties.

3. **Data Retention**
   - Messages may be temporarily stored for moderation or logging purposes.
   - Data is deleted when no longer necessary for the Bot’s operation.

4. **Security**
   - We implement reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. **Third-Party Services**
   - The Bot may rely on Discord and other services; their privacy policies apply to data processed through them.

6. **Your Rights**
   - You may stop using the Bot at any time.

7. **Changes to Privacy Policy**
   - We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of the changes.
